Providing Affordable and Free Services: “Illuminating Lives with Compassion”

At Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust, our unwavering commitment lies in ensuring that no one is deprived of basic necessities, including food, education, and medical facilities, due to financial constraints. Embodying the teachings of compassion and selfless service, we have meticulously designed a range of affordable and free services to reach out to those in need, fostering an environment of inclusivity and dignity for all.

Affordable Food Services:
Nourishing Bodies, Uplifting Spirits

FLAGSHIP SERVICES____

A Dignified Farewell:
Cremation of Unclaimed Bodies

“Embracing Souls, Fulfilling Sacred Duties”

In the sacred scriptures and the wisdom of
spiritual luminaries, the act of cremating unclaimed bodies is
considered profoundly significant. As inspired bybr the revered Kanchi
Maha Periva's teachings in the book "Deivath in Kural,
" we hold the belief that this act is akin to performing an Ashwamedha Yaga,
a noble and virtuous sacrificial rbritual of ancient times.

Cremation - A Sacred Duty

In Deivathin Kural, Maha Periva highlights the immense spiritual value of performing the last rites for unclaimed souls. He emphasizes that this selfless act is not merely an act of kindness towards the deceased, but a profound expression of our humanity and sacred duty towards all beings. Embodying the teachings of Bhagavad Gita, we undertake this service without expecting anything in return, acknowledging that true selfless service brings immeasurable blessings and fulfillment.

Identification and Documentation:

Upon receiving notification of an unclaimed body, our organization verifies the identity of the deceased individual through available documents or records, collaborating with local authorities to ensure compliance with legal requirements. Our commitment to accuracy is essential as we strive to provide dignified last rites to those with no one to claim them.

Respectful Transfer:

With a deep sense of reverence, our team ensures the dignified transfer of unclaimed bodies to our facilities. Throughout the process, the deceased is treated with the utmost dignity they deserve. We follow established protocols and are attentive to cultural or religious considerations.

Communication with Authorities:

Maintaining open communication with relevant authorities is paramount to our mission. From the moment we receive an unclaimed body, we keep the authorities informed about our procedures and progress. This collaborative approach ensures that we adhere to regulations while providing compassionate services.
