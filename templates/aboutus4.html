Embracing Humanity :

Core Values and Principles Guiding the Organization

“Compassion, Inclusivity, and Service - The Soul of Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust”

At the heart of Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust lie its core values and principles, the guiding stars that illuminate the path of compassion and service. Each value is a reflection of the trust's unwavering commitment to empowering lives, embracing souls, and creating a more equitable and compassionate world.

Compassion: Embracing the Essence of Humanity

Compassion is the very foundation on which Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust stands tall. We believe in the profound truth that each individual, regardless of circumstance, deserves to be treated with love and empathy. It is with compassion that we reach out to those in need, providing sustenance, education, and medical support, ensuring that no soul is left behind.

Inclusivity: Bridging the Divide, Embracing Unity

Inclusivity is the cornerstone of our mission. We transcend barriers of caste, creed, and community, believing that humanity knows no boundaries. We unite people from diverse backgrounds in the spirit of service, fostering a sense of togetherness that strengthens the fabric of our society.

Dignity: Respecting Every Soul's Journey

We hold the inherent dignity of every individual in the highest regard. In life and in death, we endeavor to honor each soul with respect and grace. From dignified last rites for the unclaimed to providing holistic care, we believe in upholding the sanctity of life's journey with reverence.

Empowerment: Illuminating Lives, Igniting Hope

Compassion is the very foundation on which Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust stands tall. We believe in the profound truth that each individual, regardless of circumstance, deserves to be treated with love and empathy. It is with compassion that we reach out to those in need, providing sustenance, education, and medical support, ensuring that no soul is left behind.

Integrity: Committed to Transparency and Trust

Integrity is at the core of all our actions. We strive to be transparent and accountable in all our endeavors, ensuring that our donors, partners, and beneficiaries place their trust in us with confidence. Every step we take is guided by honesty and the genuine desire to make a positive impact.

Innovation: Embracing Progress, Embodying Change

Innovation lies in our DNA. We embrace emerging technologies, modern ideas, and ancient wisdom, blending them harmoniously to create transformative solutions. Our commitment to innovation fuels our efforts to provide sustainable, cutting-edge services to those we serve.

Gratitude: Nurturing a Heart of Thankfulness

Gratitude is the soul's song of thankfulness for every blessing we receive. We are profoundly grateful to our donors, volunteers, partners, and all who join hands in our mission. It is with deep gratitude that we approach each day, knowing that together, we can create a more compassionate world.

Bhagavad Gita's Teachings: Selfless Service

The Bhagavad Gita's timeless wisdom echoes in our commitment to selfless service (Nishkama Karma). Just as Lord Krishna advises Arjuna to perform his duty without attachment to the results, we serve with boundless love and compassion, without expecting anything in return.

Ramayana's Ideals: Integrity and Dharma

The Ramayana exemplifies the virtues of integrity and adherence to Dharma (righteousness). We strive to embody these values in every action, upholding honesty and ethical conduct as we serve the community and honor the departed with dignity.

Kanchi Maha Periva's Wisdom: Compassionate Inclusivity

Kanchi Maha Periva's teachings resonate through our organization's ethos of compassionate inclusivity. His vision of a harmonious society, embracing people from all walks of life, guides our efforts to serve humanity without any discrimination.

Yogi Ramsuratkumar's Legacy: Unconditional Love

The legacy of Yogi Ramsuratkumar, the "Divine Beggar," influences our dedication to unconditional love. His life of selfless service to the needy inspires us to extend love and care to every soul we encounter, nurturing hope and healing hearts.

Gnanananda Swamigal's Simplicity: Humble Service

Gnanananda Swamigal's simplicity and humility inspire us to serve with a compassionate heart. Just as he guided seekers towards inner wisdom, we offer support to those in need, fostering empowerment and nurturing a spirit of simplicity.

Sivan Saar's Blessings: Honoring Departed Souls

Sivan Saar's divine presence and blessings echo in our unwavering commitment to honoring departed souls. Just as he shared his grace with those in his presence, we perform last rites for unclaimed individuals, ensuring they find eternal peace and dignity.

Bhagavad Gita's Call to Action: Empowerment and Courage

The Bhagavad Gita's call to action (Yogakshemam Vahamyaham) emboldens us to empower others and provide succor in times of need. We find courage in the divine assurance that the welfare and protection of those we serve is divinely guided.
